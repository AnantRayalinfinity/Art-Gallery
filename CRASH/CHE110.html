Renewable Energy Sources

Explore the various sources of renewable energy that are transforming the world.

Solar Energy

Solar energy is harnessed from the sun using photovoltaic cells.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is generated using wind turbines to convert kinetic energy into electricity.

Hydropower

Hydropower is generated from the movement of water, typically from rivers and dams.

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy is derived from the heat stored beneath the Earth's surface.
