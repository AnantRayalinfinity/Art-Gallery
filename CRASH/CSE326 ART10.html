Curious Companion

Artist: Felix Andersson

Created: 2019

Description: Swedish artist Felix Andersson’s Curious Companion is a delightful exploration of the bond between humans and animals. Created in 2019, this piece combines cubist elements with playful colors to depict a creature—half-cat, half-mystical being—who seems to be observing the viewer with keen interest. Andersson is known for his ability to blend whimsy with depth, and Curious Companion is no exception. The painting’s vibrant geometric forms and bold colors bring the creature to life, giving it a personality that is both mysterious and inviting. The piece captures the essence of curiosity and companionship, reflecting the artist’s fascination with the unique connection people share with animals. Inspired by both Picasso and traditional Scandinavian folklore, Andersson’s work combines cultural elements with a contemporary twist. Curious Companion reminds us of the joy and wonder that animals bring into our lives, while also hinting at the mystery that surrounds their inner worlds. This piece is a celebration of that quiet companionship, rendered in a style that is distinctly Andersson’s.

Medium: Acrylic on Canvas

Dimensions: 30" x 30"
